What is Toffee?

Toffee is a firm candy that is created from quality sugars and farm rich butter. While it is often called “English” Toffee, the toffee which is created by the Toffee Cottage is very American in nature. Quite often toffee has a chocolate coating added, or may be dipped in almonds or other nuts when it is made. You may know some types of toffee, such as the crunchy coating on popcorn, or the world famous Heath Bar©

How is Toffee Cottage Toffee Made?

Toffee Cottage uses quality ingredients, most of which are locally grown. You won’t find any GMO ingredients in anything that is sold by the Toffee Cottage. We strive to create our wholesome candies and sweet treats from products that are locally grown in a sustainable way. When you purchase toffee from the Toffee Cottage you can rest assured that the treats you offer your family as made as carefully and with as much attention to detail as you would offer if you created them yourself.
